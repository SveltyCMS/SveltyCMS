fix: revert wrapHandle micro-optimization that breaks CI E2E tests

The direct return wrapper (without async/await) caused page content
not to render in CI E2E tests (Config, Users, Media pages). The
microtask boundary from async/await is needed by SvelteKit's sequence
function to properly chain handlers.

Builder & Content E2E tests now pass thanks to the Playwright config
fix (baseURL port change). The remaining 9 failures across Config,
Users, and Media are pre-existing and unrelated to this change.
