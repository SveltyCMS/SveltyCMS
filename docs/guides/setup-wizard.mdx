# Setup Wizard Guide

The SveltyCMS Setup Wizard is an interactive installer that runs the first time you launch the application. It guides you through connecting your database, creating an admin account, and selecting a **Solution Preset** to jumpstart your project.

## ðŸš€ The Setup Process

### 1. Database Configuration

SveltyCMS is database-agnostic. You can choose between:

- **MongoDB** (Recommended for flexibility)
- **MariaDB / MySQL**
- **PostgreSQL** (Beta)

Enter your connection string or host details. The wizard will automatically test the connection before proceeding.

### 2. Admin Account

Create your first super-admin user. This user will have full access to all system settings.

### 3. System Settings

Configure basic site details:

- **Site Name**: Displayed in the admin bar.
- **Language**: Default system and content language.

## ðŸ“¦ Solution Presets (Starter Kits)

Presets are pre-configured collections of schemas, widgets, and settings designed for specific use cases. Selecting a preset copies standard schema files into your `config/collections` directory, saving you hours of setup time.

### Available Presets

#### 1. Blank Project

- **Best for**: Custom builds, experimental projects.
- **Includes**: Minimal configuration. No collections.

#### 2. Blog / Editorial

- **Best for**: Content-heavy sites, news portals.
- **Collections**:
  - `Posts`: Standard article schema with RichText, SEO, and Author relation.
  - `Categories`: Hierarchical organization.
  - `Authors`: Contributor profiles.

#### 3. Agency / Portfolio

- **Best for**: Design agencies, freelancers, creative studios.
- **Collections**:
  - `Projects`: Portfolio items with gallery and client details.
  - `Services`: List of offered services.
  - `Clients`: Client logos and information.
  - `Testimonials`: Social proof and reviews.

#### 4. SaaS Product

- **Best for**: Software companies, product landing pages.
- **Collections**:
  - `Pricing`: Plans managed via the **Price Widget**.
  - `Features`: Product capability highlights.
  - `Docs`: Nested documentation structure.
  - `Changelog`: Product updates.

#### 5. Corporate Site

- **Best for**: Business websites, company profiles.
- **Collections**:
  - `Team`: Leadership and staff directory.
  - `Careers`: Job openings and descriptions.
  - `Locations`: Office addresses with map coordinates.

#### 6. E-commerce

- **Best for**: Online stores, catalogs.
- **Collections**:
  - `Products`: Full catalog with **Repeater** for attributes.
  - `Orders`: Transaction records.
  - `Customers`: CRM data.
  - `Variants`: SKU management.

## ðŸ”§ Post-Setup Configuration

After the wizard completes:

1. **Log In**: Use your new admin credentials.
2. **Review Collections**: Go to the content manager to see your preset collections.
3. **Customize Schemas**: You can modify any preset collection in `config/collections/*.ts`. They are just starting points!
